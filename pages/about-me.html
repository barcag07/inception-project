Spring Semester & Future Goals
This semester has been my most difficult one so far at COM. I am taking philosophy, biology 2, programming fundamentals 2, and calculus 1. The two classes that are giving me the most trouble are biology 2 and calculus 1. Biology 2 is an interesting class, but there is a lot of memorization and a significant amount of time required for studying. Calculus isn’t difficult, but it isn’t easy either. A good amount of time is needed to fully understand what we are learning in class. I like my computer fundamentals class because it’s all programming and writing code. I am very interested in programming, so learning new concepts has been pretty easy for me so far. This philosophy class is very interesting. I took other philosophy classes at my previous school, but I enjoy this one a lot more. The main difference is that this class is more hands-on. Instead of just learning from a book, we learned how philosophy impacts the world every day with real world examples, and watching The Matrix was a cool bonus.
